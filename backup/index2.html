VALOREFY

Value your Learning. Value your Skills.

Valorefy is more than a skills gap assessment solution. We leverage the latest data science and machine learning technology to offer sophisticated education-to-job market insights. Our skills market data services and predictive models are readily consumable via API for integration into your existing systems. We offer Data Science as a Service (DSaaS) solutions for education providers as well as HR, employee training and staffing organizations.

Valorefy: Tracking the Skills Economy

Valorefy is a Kaplan Education solution. Our patented skills processing technology is inspired by over 75 years of learning science and student-centric innovation.
