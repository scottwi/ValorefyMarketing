HR, Employee Training and Staffing Solutions

People are the most important assets of today’s information driven economy. Ensuring that your organization counts with the right skills and talent pool to move forward is therefore vital to success. Whether you are hiring or training internal resources, Valorefy can help you match people to skills and skills to jobs or continuing education opportunities. We offer the following data products:

  • Resume Skills Advisor

    Input a resume and automatically extract explicit as well related skills. Works as a resume optimization or resume evaluation tool to determine fit with a given job post.

    Course Skills Advisor

    Input a course description or curriculum and automatically extract a list of reinforced skills. Serves as an evaluation tool for continued education courses. Administrators may contrast and compare different education options to fulfill a given set of employee training needs.

    Skills Match

    Compare any two texts for similar skills. This is the preferred service for skill gap assessment. Examples include comparisons between two job posts (for job post optimization) , job posts and resumes/cover letters, job posts and course content, etc.

  • Career and Jobs Match

    Match any set of skills to best fitting jobs. Relate output with geographical and salary distribution of job posts for refined statistics about employability and location of career opportunities.

    Skills Ladder

    Find the skills required to take your employees from point A to point B in training and sequence the courses required for their individual educational journeys.

    MOOC Search

    Search for open courses related to the skills you want to reinforce. Combine this tool with any of the skill assessment feeds mentioned above for a complete hire to retire employee training roadmap.

Education Providers Solutions

Return on Educational Investment (ROEI) is one of the most talked about yet elusive objectives of modern higher education. Education providers want to know how their curriculum, learning materials and experiences are not just enriching their students’ lives, but providing them with the skills to succeed in the job market. Valorefy provides education leaders with unique insights about their programs’ alignment with market demands and general employment trends. We offer the following data products:

  • Resume Skills Advisor

    Input a resume and automatically extract explicit as well related skills. Works as a resume optimization tool for student advising or career resource departments helping students highlight their strengths against their desired jobs.

    Course Skills Advisor

    Input a course description or learning material and automatically extract a list of reinforced skills. Supports curriculum evaluation efforts towards graduate employability and workforce readiness. Administrators may contrast and compare different learning material options to fulfill a given set of job market needs.

    Skills Match

    Automatically compare any two texts for similar skills. This is the preferred service for competency and skill gap assessment. This service can be seamlessly integrated into any admissions system tasked with translating experiential or prior learning into college credit.

  • Career and Jobs Match

    Match any set of skills to best fitting jobs. Relate output with geographical and salary distribution of job posts for refined statistics about employability and location of career opportunities. This data service is an ideal supplement to existing student advising and career resource systems in your school.

    Skills Ladder

    Find the skills required to take your students from point A to point B in their their individual educational journeys. Help them sequence electives and extracurricular opportunities to fulfill the skill demands of their dream career.

    MOOC Search

    Search for open courses related to the skills you want to reinforce beyond your classroom. Combine this tool with any of the skill assessment feeds mentioned above to support a competency based program where outside resources are combined with a formal educational program.

  • Transfer Credit Matching

    Compare courses from different institutions to assess similarities and potential credit equivalences. This is an ideal tool for busy admissions and registrar departments looking to streamline their student transfer operations and prior learning management systems.
